Hurling

Details: Hurling is a field sport played mostly in Ireland. A hurling game consists of two teams for 15 players who use hurleys made out of wood to try and put the ball over the bar and between thw uprights for one point or in to the back of the net for three points. The only protective gear hurlers wear is a helmet to protect their head. In my eyes hurling is the perfect balance of power and skill and for these reasons hurling is my favourite sport. The hurling team that i support is Cork, last year i did not miss one Cork championship game including the Munster final which they won, after that game we stormed the pitch and the atmosphere was absolutly electric. I currently play hurling with Fermoy G.A.A. at under 21 and Intermediate level. We usually train two nights a week, Monday and Friday.
