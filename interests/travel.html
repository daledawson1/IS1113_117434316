Most travelled too: Spain

I have travelled to spain three times in my life so far, I have been in Malaga, Madrid and Barcelona. Out of the three places my favourite place was Barcelona because of the beautiful scenery and the amazing culture in the area.

The spainish soccer team is one of the most successful in the world they have won the world cup one time in the competitions history. The domestic teams within spain are amongst the best in the world with Barcelona winning the club super cup in 2014 and 2016 and Real Madrid winning the same competition in 2015 and in 2017.
